## Ingredients

1. Pasta
1. Mayo
1. Dijon Mustard
1. Celery
1. Carrots
1. Peas (i.e. regular or edamame)
1. Salt
1. Pepper

### Protein

1. Tuna

## Instructions

1. Cook the pasta + peas
1. Mix the dressing
   1. 2 tbsp Mayo
   1. 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
   1. Salt
   1. Pepper
1. Toss together
